How Cases Work
The Case Format

Every case follows the same four-step structure.

01
Clinical History
Patient demographics, relevant oncologic or neurologic history, and the clinical question — staging, restaging, theranostic eligibility, or response assessment.
02
Blinded Images
MIP, axial PET, and fused images without the answer. Full DICOM stack available via PACSbin link. Form your impression first.
03
Findings & Diagnosis
Structured findings with SUVmax, annotated key image, and a reveal-on-click diagnosis with tracer mechanism and clinical significance.
04
Teaching Pearls
3–5 high-yield takeaways, differential table, key references, and a downloadable PowerPoint for resident teaching sessions.
